# Olist E-Commerce Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the Olist Brazilian E-Commerce Dataset using PostgreSQL, SQL, and Power BI.

The objective was to uncover business insights related to revenue performance, customer behavior, product category sales, and seller performance.

---

## Tools & Technologies

* PostgreSQL
* SQL
* Power BI
* GitHub

---

## Dataset Information

The dataset contains:

* 99,441 Orders
* 96,096 Customers
* 112,650 Order Items
* 103,886 Payments
* 32,951 Products
* 3,095 Sellers

Source: Olist Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset

---

## Business Questions Solved

### Revenue Analysis

* What is the total revenue generated?
* What is the average order value?
* How does revenue change over time?

### Customer Analysis

* How many customers are active?
* What percentage of customers return and place multiple orders?
* Which states generate the highest revenue?

### Product Analysis

* Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
* Which categories contribute most to the business?

### Seller Analysis

* Which seller cities generate the highest revenue?
* How concentrated is seller performance?

## Key Insights

### Revenue

* Total marketplace revenue exceeded 16 million.
* Revenue showed strong growth throughout 2017 and peaked during 2018.

### Customers

* Repeat customer rate was approximately 3.12%.
* Most customers placed only one order.

### Geography

* São Paulo (SP) generated the highest revenue by a large margin.
* Rio de Janeiro (RJ) and Minas Gerais (MG) followed.

### Products

Top-performing categories:

1. Beauty & Health
2. Watches & Gifts
3. Home & Bath
4. Sports & Leisure
5. Tech Accessories

### Sellers

Seller revenue was concentrated among a small number of cities, with São Paulo dominating marketplace activity.
